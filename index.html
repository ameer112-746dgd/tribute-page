Wizkid: Starboy

Ayodeji Ibrahim Balogun, known professionally as Wizkid, is a Nigerian singer and songwriter. He started his music career at the age of 11 and rose to fame in 2010 with the release of his debut album "Superstar".

Discography

  • Superstar (2010)
  • Ayo (2014)
  • Sounds from the Other Side (2017)
  • Made in Lagos (2020)

Awards and Nominations

  • BET Awards: Best New Artist (2012)
  • MOBO Awards: Best African Act (2016)
  • Grammy Awards: Best World Music Album (2021)
